WHY FPGA

Why do AI Perception Systems Need FPGA Preprocessing?

In AI vision and audio applications, significant time and computing resources are consumed during the data preprocessing stage, such as image conversion, noise reduction, feature extraction, and signal conversion. Relying solely on a CPU or GPU for processing easily creates latency and power bottlenecks, affecting real-time response capabilities. FPGA can complete real-time preprocessing before data enters AI models through hardware parallelism and pipeline design, significantly reducing latency and improving overall system performance.
