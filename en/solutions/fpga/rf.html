WHY FPGA

Why do RF Systems Need FPGA?

RF systems need to process high-speed, high-bandwidth, and real-time data streams, with extremely high demands on latency, throughput, and computing efficiency.

Relying on its reconfigurable and highly parallelized architecture, FPGA has become the ideal computing platform for RF and wireless communication systems.

Core Advantages

  • Real-time high-speed signal processing
  • Highly parallelized computing architecture
  • Low Latency processing capability
  • Reconfigurable and highly elastic
  • Supports High-Speed Interfaces (PCIe, JESD204B/C, 100GbE)
  • Suitable for SDR (Software-Defined Radio)
