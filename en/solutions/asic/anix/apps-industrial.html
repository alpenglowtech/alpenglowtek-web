SMART MANUFACTURING

Smart manufacturing and predictive maintenance

In automated production, full-time monitoring is the key to ensuring stability, but traditional solutions are often limited by high power consumption and latency.

ANIX implements extremely low power consumption predictive maintenance through real-time reasoning at the edge, effectively reducing the risk of downtime and significantly improving operational energy efficiency.

EDGE MONITORING

Decentralized edge monitoring and intelligent filtering

Wide-area distributed sensing nodes are often deployed in power-constrained areas, and uploading a full amount of data will quickly exhaust battery life.

ANIX performs intelligent filtering locally, extracting and transmitting only key event patterns. This mechanism greatly extends the equipment inspection life and enables more immediate abnormality alerts.

ALWAY-ON SENSING

Micro terminals and always-aware applications

Micro devices need to maintain smart functions within a very small energy consumption budget, and "always on call" is usually the biggest power burden.

ANIX provides ultra-low power consumption "Always-on" capabilities, quickly completes pattern matching at the edge, and greatly improves device autonomy without affecting battery life.
